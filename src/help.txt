VALID OPERATIONS
- Mathematics:
    - "+" for addition
    - "-" for subtraction, where x and y are the subtrahend and minuend, respectively
    - "*" for multiplication
    - "/" for division, where x and y are the divisor and dividend, respectively
- Stack management:
    - "clear" or "c" to clear all the elements
    - "drop" or "d" to drop the top (xth) element
    - "roll" or "r" to roll the top (xth) element to the bottom
    - "swap" or "s" to swap the top (xth) and second-to-top (yth) elements

MISCELLANEOUS NOTES
- Enter "quit" or "q" to force quit the application
- The maximum stack length is 100
- After five invalid inputs, the application will shut down
